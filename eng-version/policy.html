Refund Policy

Customer satisfaction is one of our Company's top priorities. Therefore, we offer customers the option to apply for a refund if they are not satisfied with our professional assistance services.

If you wish to request a refund, please complete and submit the contact form via the link below, clearly stating the reason for your refund request:

Contact Form

The refund request will be evaluated within 24 hours of receipt. Our Customer Service Department will review the reasons and specific circumstances of your case to determine whether the refund can be approved or denied. In any case, you will be informed of the outcome of your request.

Refund Request Deadline

Refund requests must be submitted within 120 days of receiving our email confirming successful payment for our professional services. After this period, refunds will not be approved, except in exceptional cases based on the individual circumstances of the customer.

Remarks:

  • If you are requesting a refund for multiple orders of Electronic Travel Documents, each request must be submitted individually.
  • Customers whose Electronic Travel Document has not been submitted to the Government by the date of the refund request will be eligible for a full refund.
  • Customers whose Electronic Travel Document application has been approved by the Government are eligible for a refund of our service fee. If a refund request is accepted for an approved application, the Government fee for the assessment of the application is non-refundable, except in particular cases depending on the customer's circumstances.
  • Customers whose Electronic Travel Document application has been denied by the Government are eligible for a full refund upon request, in accordance with our professional service guarantee and the particular circumstances of their case.
  • Ancillary service costs are non-refundable under any circumstances.

Refund Process

If your refund request is accepted by the Customer Service Department:

  • The refund will be issued within 72 hours from the receipt of the request, using the same payment method you used to pay for our professional services.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been processed.
  • Depending on the payment entity, the refunded amount may take up to ten (10) business days to be credited to your account.
