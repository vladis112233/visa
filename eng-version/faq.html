Dominican e-Ticket Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Dominican e-Ticket?

The Dominican e-Ticket is an electronic ticket. All passengers must follow this step to enter and leave the Dominican Republic.

The Dominican entry and exit e-Ticket is not a visa. You should check the visa requirements for your nationality before your trip.

Depending on your nationality, you may need both an e-Ticket and a visa to enter the Dominican Republic.

Do I need an e-Ticket for the Dominican Republic?
Yes, if you are traveling to the Dominican Republic you must have an approved Dominican e-Ticket or you cannot enter. You also need an exit e-Ticket to leave the country.
How long can I stay in the Dominican Republic with an e-Ticket?

The Dominican e-Ticket is not a visa. The time you can stay in the country depends on your nationality and whether you need a visa to enter.

Visa-exempt passport holders can stay in the Dominican Republic for up to 90 days. There are over 100 nations that can travel visa-free.

All other travelers must obtain a visa before arrival, in addition to the Dominican e-Ticket.

When does a Dominican e-Ticket expire?
Applicants must enter their travel information when they complete the Dominican e-Ticket online form. This means that the e-Ticket is only valid on the date that was stated in the application.
What do I need to apply for a Dominican e-Ticket?
You just need your passport, an email address, and a credit or debit card to apply for the Dominican Republic e-Ticket.
Do children need a Dominican e-Ticket to travel to the Dominican Republic?

All passengers must have a valid e-Ticket to visit the Dominican Republic, this includes children of all ages.

Dominican e-Ticket applications can be grouped together, with a maximum of 7 passengers on each application. You will have the option to state if you are traveling with a group, and then to add each person’s details.

I am a transit passenger, do I need to apply for a Dominican e-Ticket?
Yes, the Dominican Republic e-Ticket is mandatory for anyone entering or leaving the country, even for a stopover.
I am a Dominican resident, do I need to apply for a Dominican e-Ticket?
Residents of the Dominican Republic must also have an e-Ticket. There is an option on the application form to state your residence details.
How often do I need to apply for a Dominican e-Ticket?
You must apply for a Dominican e-Ticket every time you enter and leave the Dominican Republic. The QR code for each e-Ticket is only valid for use once.
Can I work in the Dominican Republic with an approved Dominican e-Ticket?
The e-Ticket is not a work permit or a visa. To work in the Dominican Republic you must obtain a valid work permit.
What do I do if I have two (2) passports?
You must use the same passport to complete the e-Ticket application and to enter or leave the Dominican Republic.
How can I find out my Dominican e-Ticket application status?
All updates and notifications for your Dominican e-Ticket application are sent to you by email. You can find out the status of your e-Ticket by checking your email inbox.
How can I make the Dominican e-Ticket application payment?
All major payment methods are accepted for the Dominican e-Ticket application fee. You can pay by credit or debit card securely online. If you experience any issues with payment, please reach us at info@eticketfordominicana.com
What is the CVV/CVC/CVC2 number on your credit or debit card?
The CVV/CVC/CVC2 number is a 3 or 4-digit number on the back of your credit or debit card. For American Express, the CVV number is on the front of the card, above the account number.
I submitted my application for the Dominican e-Ticket and I still haven’t received a confirmation, what should I do?
Applicants receive a confirmation email when they have submitted the form. Please check your email inbox and spam folder before contacting us via email at info@eticketfordominicana.com
What do I do if my Dominican e-Ticket is denied?
The decision to approve or deny a Dominican e-Ticket is at the discretion of the government authorities. In the rare case that an application is rejected, you can contact us to request a full refund.
I’ve realized that some information on my Dominican e-Ticket is incorrect, what should I do?
We strongly advise you to check your information before submitting the form for the Dominican e-Ticket. Any mistakes or false information could lead to a rejected e-Ticket application. If you have already submitted your application and realized that it contains incorrect information, please contact us immediately at info@eticketfordominicana.com
