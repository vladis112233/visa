Information About the Dominican e-Ticket

About the Dominican e-Ticket

The Dominican e-Ticket is an electronic ticket that is mandatory for all passengers, whether Dominican or foreign, entering or leaving the Dominican Republic.

The e-Ticket has 3 parts:

  • International Embarkation/Disembarkation forms: Details about your entry or exit from the country.
  • Traveler’s Health Affidavit: Information about your health status.
  • Customs Declaration: A declaration of goods being carried into the country.

You can get the Dominican e-Ticket by completing the online application form with your personal details, travel information, and passport data.

Once approved, the e-Ticket will be sent to you by email. Ensure that you present the e-Ticket to border control upon arrival for validation.

Dominican e-Ticket Entry Specifications and Validity

A Dominican e-Ticket is only valid for the date of arrival or departure stated on your application. Each e-Ticket is valid for a single use. You must have a separate e-Ticket for each entry and exit to the Dominican Republic.

Dominican e-Ticket Information

The Dominican e-Ticket is required for entering and exiting the Dominican Republic. Here's everything you need to know about designated airports, documentation, ticket types, and the application process.

You can enter and exit the Dominican Republic through these designated airports by presenting your Dominican e-Ticket, passport, and other required documents:

  • Las Américas International Airport
  • Punta Cana International Airport
  • Cibao International Airport
  • Gregorio Luperón International Airport
  • La Isabela International Airport
  • La Romana International Airport
  • María Montez International Airport
  • Presidente Juan Bosh International Airport
  • San Isidro Air Base

Documentation Requirements

To apply for the Dominican e-Ticket, you'll need the following:

  • A valid passport from one of the eligible countries.
  • A credit or debit card to pay the e-Ticket fees online.
Check your email for the confirmation email with the subject INFORMATION NEEDED - Order Confirmation to access your profile.

Types of Dominican e-Tickets

Choose the type of e-Ticket based on your travel needs:

  1. Arrival and Departure from the Dominican Republic: Suitable for round-trip passengers, allowing for easier return travel.
  2. Arrival in the Dominican Republic: For passengers entering the Dominican Republic without confirmed return travel or residents.
  3. Departure from the Dominican Republic: For passengers or residents leaving the country.

Online Application

Completing the Dominican e-Ticket application online is quick and easy. You'll need to provide:

  • Personal details (first and last name, place and date of birth, nationality, gender).
  • Passport information.
  • Travel details, accommodation details, and health and customs information.
