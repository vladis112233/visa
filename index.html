Dominican e-Ticket

All visitors to the Dominican Republic must have a Dominican e-Ticket to enter and leave the country.

The e-Ticket acts as an entry and exit card for each passenger. It is also a Customs Declaration Form and a Travel Health Affidavit.

Without completing this mandatory step, you cannot enter the Dominican Republic. You will also not be permitted to leave the country if you do not have a valid e-Ticket to exit.

You can get your Dominican e-Ticket online by completing a simple form. Applicants then receive the approved e-Ticket by email.

Dominican e-Ticket

The Dominican e-Ticket application form is 100% online. You can access the form from any electronic device with an internet connection.

You only need to provide some basic personal information and details about your trip.

You will receive your approved Dominican e-Ticket by email in 1 to 2 days.

The e-Ticket is not a visa. You must also follow the Dominican Republic’s visa policy for your nationality.

You can contact a Dominican embassy or consulate for more information on its visa policy.

If you are not visa-exempt, you must have both a Dominican e-Ticket and a valid visa to travel to the Dominican Republic.
